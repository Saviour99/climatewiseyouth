About ClimateWISEYouth

Our Mission

ClimateWiseYouth is a global platform dedicated to empowering young people to take meaningful action against climate change. We believe that youth are not just the leaders of tomorrowâ€”they are the changemakers of today.

Founded in 2020, we've grown into a vibrant community of passionate young activists, educators, and innovators working together to create a sustainable future for all.

Our Values

Innovation

We embrace creative solutions and cutting-edge approaches to tackle climate challenges.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of working together across borders, cultures, and communities.

Justice

We advocate for climate justice and equity, ensuring vulnerable communities are heard.

Action

We turn awareness into action through concrete initiatives and measurable impact.
